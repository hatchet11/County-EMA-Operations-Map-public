# Marion County EMA — Operations Map

### A live emergency-management situational-awareness map for an entire county.

**Live:** [mcema.us](https://mcema.us) · **Status:** In production, serving Marion County, Illinois · **Seeking:** sponsorship/donations, govtech adoption, and partners to expand it to other counties

---

## What it is

A public, real-time operations and situational-awareness map for county emergency management. In one place, residents and responders can see active operations, weather alerts, road closures, burn status, and the critical-infrastructure and hazard picture for the county — on any device, instantly.

## What it shows
- **Live National Weather Service alerts** for the county (automatic).
- **Active operations, road closures, and burn status**, posted by EMA staff.
- **Critical infrastructure** and **response assets** layers.
- **Vulnerable-population** and **environmental-hazard** layers for planning and response.
- **Seismic awareness** — New Madrid and Wabash Valley seismic zones, historic coal-mine and liquefaction context, with a live USGS earthquake feed and automatic operational triggers for significant nearby events.
- **Parcel and FIRM (flood) reference** layers.

## Why it matters

Most counties have no shared, live common operating picture — information lives in radios, emails, and PDFs. This map gives the public and responders the same real-time view, built on a fast, low-cost, resilient edge architecture that any county can afford. It is a model for **affordable, modern civic emergency-management technology**.
